<p><p>The industrial linear valves market comprises various types, including gate valves, diaphragm valves, and others. Gate valves are primarily used for on/off control and provide minimal flow resistance when fully open. Diaphragm valves are ideal for applications requiring leak-proof sealing and flow regulation, often found in sanitary and corrosive processes. Other types include globe valves and pinch valves, each serving specific needs in fluid control across industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemicals.</p></p>
