<p><p>The portable salinity refractometer market consists primarily of two types: analog and digital salinity refractometers. Analog salinity refractometers utilize a simple optical system with a calibrated scale to measure salinity levels, providing a straightforward, no-power-required solution. In contrast, digital salinity refractometers employ advanced sensors and electronic displays to deliver more precise measurements and often include features like data logging and temperature compensation. Both types are widely used in aquaculture, environmental monitoring, and marine research for effective salinity assessment.</p></p>
