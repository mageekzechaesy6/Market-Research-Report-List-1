<p><p>In Litecoin exchanges, market types such as Pay To Public Key Hash (P2PKH), Pay To Public Key (P2PK), and Pay To Script Hash (P2SH) define how transactions are constructed and validated. P2PKH involves sending coins to a hashed public key, ensuring security and anonymity. P2PK directly transfers coins to a public key, providing simpler verification. P2SH enables complex transactions by allowing funds to be sent to a script hash, enabling multi-signature capabilities and advanced contract conditions.</p></p>
