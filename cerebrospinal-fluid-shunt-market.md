<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cerebrospinal Fluid Shunt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventriculoperitoneal (VP)</li><li>Ventriculoatrial (VA)</li><li>Lumboperitoneal (LP)</li><li>Ventriculopleural (VPL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cerebrospinal fluid shunt market comprises various types, including Ventriculoperitoneal (VP), Ventriculoatrial (VA), Lumboperitoneal (LP), and Ventriculopleural (VPL) shunts. VP shunts divert excess cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) to the peritoneal cavity, commonly used for hydrocephalus. VA shunts direct CSF to the atrium of the heart. LP shunts channel CSF from the lumbar region to the peritoneal cavity, while VPL shunts route CSF to the pleural cavity, offering diverse solutions for managing CSF-related disorders effectively.</p></p>
