<p><p>Human Hepatitis B Immunoglobulin (HBIG) is a sterile solution containing antibodies derived from human plasma, specifically designed to provide passive immunity against Hepatitis B virus (HBV) infection. It is primarily used in post-exposure prophylaxis for individuals who have been exposed to HBV, particularly in cases of perinatal transmission and after exposure in healthcare settings. Beyond its immediate protective capabilities, HBIG plays a critical role in managing patients undergoing liver transplantation who are at high risk for HBV reactivation.</p><p>The Human Hepatitis B Immunoglobulin market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of Hepatitis B, rising healthcare expenditure, and enhanced screening programs. The growing prevalence of HBV infections globally, coupled with expanding immunization programs, significantly boosts demand for HBIG. Furthermore, advances in manufacturing processes and the development of new therapeutic applications are shaping the market landscape. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period, reflecting a robust trajectory as the healthcare sector increasingly prioritizes prevention and early intervention strategies against Hepatitis B. Additionally, the rising number of liver transplants and improvements in healthcare infrastructure in emerging economies are contributing to the overall market expansion.</p></p>
