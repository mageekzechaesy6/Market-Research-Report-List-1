<p><p>The polyethylene (PE) heat shrink films market consists primarily of LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) heat shrink films. LDPE heat shrink films are known for their flexibility, softness, and ease of sealing, making them suitable for packaging a variety of products. In contrast, LLDPE heat shrink films offer enhanced strength, puncture resistance, and stretchability, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and performance. Both types find extensive use in packaging, ensuring protection and presentation for various goods.</p></p>
